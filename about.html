About Me

From Developer to Deal-Maker

Rishabh started his career as a Salesforce Technical Analyst at TCS in 2016 — writing code, managing incidents, and figuring out how Salesforce actually works under the hood. What he discovered along the way was that his real strength wasn't in building the product. It was in explaining it, positioning it, and helping clients see its value.

That realisation led him to Presales — and he never looked back. Over the next several years, he worked across TCS, Brillio, and LTIMindtree, shaping Salesforce solutions for clients across Finance, Automobile, Realty, Hospitality, and Retail sectors, spanning EMEA, APAC, and the US.

Today, Rishabh works at Salesforce Professional Services as a Salesforce Consultant, helping grow the Data Cloud, Marketing Cloud, and RCG Cloud implementation business across India — collaborating with C-suite stakeholders, building winning proposals, and connecting the right solutions to the right problems.

He also holds an MBA in Marketing, AI & Machine Learning from Great Lakes Institute of Management — bringing both technical grounding and a sharp commercial lens to every Salesforce conversation.

🌍 The TCS Middle East Milestone

During his time at TCS, Rishabh contributed to expanding the Salesforce practice into the EMEA region — collaborating with cross-domain teams to drive growth and helping establish TCS's Salesforce presence in new geographies.

📈 Growing PS India's Pipeline

At Salesforce Professional Services, Rishabh single-handedly managed Data Cloud, Marketing Cloud, and RCG Cloud deals and helped acquire three new logos for PS India — from discovery to commercial close.

🎓 Research & Curiosity

His MBA thesis explored the effect of nostalgia on consumer buying behaviour — an early signal of his fascination with what actually drives decisions. That thinking continues to shape how he approaches client conversations.
